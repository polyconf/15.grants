PolyConf 2015
Opportunity Grants

We are pleased to announce our Opportunity Grant which aims to support community members who would not otherwise be able to attend the conference or training for financial reasons.

Eligibility Grants are available for all traditionally under-represented groups in technology. A preference will be given to applicants based in Europe. By submitting an application, you confirm that you are eligible under these criteria.

Selection Applications will be reviewed by the committee and will be accepted based upon need and impact. All information will be kept confidential.

Award Applicants will receive a conference registration ticket and, if awarded, travel assistance along with a workshop ticket. If you are awarded a grant, you agree that you will attend all three days of the conference (July 2-4).

Notification All applicants will be notified via email with conference registration details and travel reimbursement details.

Application Fill out the Opportunity Grant application. Application information will be reviewed and notification will be distributed on a rolling basis.

All conference and event attendees are expected to adhere to the PolyConf's Code of Conduct.
